The seven-step loop

Tap each stage. Watch the trust build.

1. Post your task

Choose service type, complexity, and mode. Rush costs a small surcharge — standard is free to post. The task lands on the Panabo community board within your 2 km radius.

2. A verified neighbor accepts

The task flashes for 15 seconds to nearby online Ka-Bayans. The first verified worker to accept takes it. You see their name, trust score, and distance instantly.

3. Entrance face scan

Worker arrives. Start Task stays locked until their face scan matches the ID on file. No match, no start — no exceptions.

4. Task execution

GPS keeps a live breadcrumb inside your radius. Low-battery? System fires a location ping at 1% as a final fail-safe.

5. Evidence capture

Before and after photos taken live — camera-only, watermarked with location and time, scanned by AI for tampering or moiré patterns.

6. Exit face scan

Finish Task is locked until the second scan confirms the same person who started also finished. This is how fake completions die.

7. Payment & rating

Payment releases to the worker's wallet minus the 10% service fee. You rate the job. A 30-minute dispute window stays open just in case.
